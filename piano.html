Klavier - Lernen in VR

This serious game was created during my bachelor's thesis.
The game was developed for the Oculus Quest system and uses the hand-recognition to allows the users to control the ingame hands simply by moving their real hands.

The game allows you to play training sessions and songs. In training sessions the notes you have to press pause when you are to slow, to allow learning at your own speed.
In song mode the notes thath are pressed too late are counted as mistakes.
You can add your own songs to the game to be able to learn playing piano with your favourite songs!

The game was developed using the Unity-Engine.
